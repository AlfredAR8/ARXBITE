App Functionality

Spotify Analysis

We connect to your Spotify account to read your top tracks and artists. This allows us to visualize your listening trends over time.

AI Insights

Using OpenAI (ChatGPT), we generate unique, personalized descriptions of your music persona based on your actual history.

Playlist Creation

Discover new music and save it directly to your library. We require permission to create public or private playlists on your behalf.

Data Usage & Transparency

We value your privacy. Echo Insight requests specific permissions solely to provide the core functionality of the app. We do not sell your data.

Read User Top Items Used to analyze your most listened-to tracks and artists to generate your music profile.
Playlist Modify (Public/Private) Required only so the app can create new playlists in your account when you ask it to.
User Read Email Used solely for unique user identification within our secure database.
