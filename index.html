SkyDome

Transform your room into a stunning skyscape with Sky Dome. Using your Meta Quest Headset, Sky Dome scans your ceiling and allows you to replace it with a variety of immersive skies, from star-filled nights to serene, cloud-filled days. You can even upload your own 360° images for a personalized experience. Whether you're looking to relax, boost creativity, or simply elevate your space, Sky Dome brings a peaceful or inspiring atmosphere to any room. Plus, you can continue using 2D apps on the universal menu while enjoying your new sky.
